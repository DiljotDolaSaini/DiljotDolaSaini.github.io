My Introduction

Hi, my name is Diljot Dola Saini and currently I am a 3rd year Electrical Engineering student at McMaster University. As an electrical engineer my main interests are power electronics and embedded systems, however, I have a profound interest in coding and web development and I seek opportunitites to put these skills to use and further enhance them.







Over the years I have personally seen myself evolve as an engineer as I try to educate myself in multiple disciplines to solve all types of problems, because this is what engineering means to me! In the industry my goal is to innovate and create successful technology for the advancment of our civilization.
